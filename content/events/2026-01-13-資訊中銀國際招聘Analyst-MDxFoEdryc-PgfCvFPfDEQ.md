中銀國際 BOCI

The Role

Analyst, Junior Treasury Dealer, Fixed Income

Key Accountabilities

Assist in the Group's daily treasury functions which include forecasting, monitoring and controlling cash management activities;Assist in executing the Group's FTP mechanism;Assist in daily dealing activities including internal and external FX dealing;Assist in the regular review of credit limits and bank facilities;Prepare reports in relation to treasury dealing activities;Assist in setting up and the annual review of Group treasury related policies, guidelines, procedures and manuals;Assist in providing necessary support and information to front offices / related back offices in relation to Group treasury;Such other duties as shall be directed by your superiors from time to time.

Skills & Experience

Degree holder in Economics / Finance / Risk Management / Information System or equivalent; with a recognized professional qualification (such as CFA, FRM or equivalent) preferred;Minimum 2 years of full time working experience in banks / financial institutions in Hong Kong or overseas is considered as an advantage; Exposure in ALM / treasury management / IT project management an added advantage;Strong product knowledge in treasury market;Proficient in MS Office; Knowledge in VBA an added advantage;A team player, self-starter, mature, detailed oriented and able to work under pressure; excellent figures sense;Excellent interpersonal and communication skills;Good command of spoken and written English and Chinese.

Other Information

Please apply in strict confidence with full resume, academic record, current and expected salaries;The personal data provided will be used for consideration of recruitment only. All personal data of unsuccessful candidate will be destroyed within 24 months;Candidates with Enhanced Competency Framework (ECF):  please state on the CV.
